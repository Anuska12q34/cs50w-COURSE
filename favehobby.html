WHY READING IS MY FIRST LOVE


I have loved reading even before i knew I loved it. It has always been something that been comforting to me. From finishing all school books the day they were newly bought to reading them till 4 am . The subject itself didn't matter much , but what did matter was that every subject was somehow a story written differently.

And so I let it go

And while I still love reading , i don't read like i used to anymore. And just like every human who has unknowing let the thing they love the most , slip away. I did too

And so this is my story of my first love and what makes me.
